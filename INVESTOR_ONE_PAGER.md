## The Problem

Software became visual. Founders, SaaS teams, and product marketers need launch videos, product reveals, social clips, App Store previews, and onboarding visuals — every single week.

But premium motion design is:

- **Expensive** — $15K+ per launch
- **Slow** — 2 weeks of revisions per cycle
- **Unscalable** — every feature needs a brief, designer, and approval loop

So most companies ship incredible products and announce them with screen recordings, generic trailers, and rushed edits that don't match the quality of the work that went in.

## The Solution

Videly AI is the **AI motion designer** for modern startups. Upload screenshots, paste a short script, and get a cinematic, launch-ready video in minutes.

**4-step workflow:**

1. **Upload your product** — screenshots, UI flows, or product URLs
2. **Tell the story** — short script, release notes, or feature bullets
3. **Generate cinematic motion** — AI builds scenes, transitions, pacing, and animations automatically
4. **Export everywhere** — X, LinkedIn, Product Hunt, App Store, websites, ads

No editing timeline. No After Effects. No motion designer required.
